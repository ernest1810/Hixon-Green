Hixon Green is an all-day destination, offering coffee, breakfast, brunch, lunch, dinner and a full drinks menu. Everything is produced on site, by our experienced & talented chefs, putting a modern spin on traditional flavours.

We offer brunch and lunch until 3pm everyday (brunch only menu at weekends) before handing our kitchen over to our very talented team of Punjabi chefs cooking up an Indian storm every evening.

For our Indian menu, we offer a call and collect service – sorry we do not offer a delivery service.

Please call the restaurant direct to place your order

 

 

Barfield Butchers:
Situated in the heart of Fiveways in Brighton, Barfields is a real butchers. We source the finest in free range naturally reared meat from Sussex and nearby counties.

Jacobs Cakes:
Jacob has been baking his amazing cakes and creations since 2002. We offer gluten-free and vegan options alongside his indulgent cakes and sweet treats.

MCB Seafoods:
Is a family run business that has been operating out of Newhaven, East Sussex for over 25 years. With sustainability as its’ core approach they only sell the freshest fish meaning it goes from sea to saucepan with a high quality end result every time.

Backyard Coffee:
Backyard Coffee are an independent coffee roaster based in Ditchling, Sussex. Backyard buy only beans they can trust in and over the years have put a lot of effort and time in selecting our beans ensuring that they are fair trade and ethically sourced.

Brownie Points:
Our delicious brownies are produced by a husband and wife team in Shoreham. We promise you have NEVER tried anything like them and you’ll be hooked!

Enotria & Coe:
Are the UK’s leading wine and spirits supplier, focused on building strong partnerships with customers, producers and brands to produce results. They are passionate about what they do and what you drink, making them unique from the rest.
